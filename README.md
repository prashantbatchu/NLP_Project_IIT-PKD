# Bihar 2025 Election Analysis

**Data Science Internship Project**  
**Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad**

Comparative analysis between political leaders' speeches and official manifestos of **NDA** and **Mahagathbandhan** for Bihar Assembly Elections 2025.

### Objective
To analyze how well election promises in manifestos are reflected in public speeches using NLP and manual annotation techniques.

### Data
- 4 Major Speeches: Narendra Modi (Chhapra), Amit Shah (Lakhisarai), Tejashwi Yadav (Karakat), Rahul Gandhi & Tejashwi (Muzaffarpur)
- 2 Official Manifestos: NDA Sankalp Patra & Mahagathbandhan Manifesto

### Methodology
- Audio to Text using `yt-dlp` + Whisper Medium model
- Manual transcription correction and error tracking
- Defined 10 thematic categories (Employment, Women Empowerment, Infrastructure, etc.)
- Manual topic tagging of manifestos and speeches
- Comparative frequency analysis

- Mahagathbandhan emphasized Employment and Social Justice
